# Suggested Features & Future Roadmap

This roadmap compiles all features, mechanics, items, and design directions that were suggested or planned during the development history of the **Breakfast Addon** but have not yet been implemented or investigated.

---

## 1. Culinary Expansion (Drinks, Baked Goods, & Meats)

These culinary additions were suggested in the **v1.1 roadmap** and **original specifications** to expand the early-game food progression.

### ☕ Beverages
* **Coffee & Tea**: Hot breakfast beverages providing localized player status effects (e.g., Speed or Haste).
* **Orange Juice**: A cold, fruit-based drink crafted from future citrus crops or sweet berries.

### 🧁 Baked Goods
* **Blueberry Muffins**: Sweet berry and wheat-based baked goods.
* **Cinnamon Rolls**: High-nutrition dessert pastry.
* **Bagels**: Savory grain-based breakfast bread option.

### 🥓 Expanded Pork & Meat Options
* **Pig Fat**: A primary drop from pigs. Can be cooked directly, sliced on the butcher block, or rendered into Lard.
* **Sausage Links**: A secondary sausage variant (distinct from the current generic Sausage item).
* **Country Ham**: A cured/aged ham item providing a longer-duration food alternative.

### 🍯 Raw Ingredients (From Original Spec)
* **Berry Mash** (`breakfast:berry_mash`): Raw processed sweet berries.
* **Glow Jam** (`breakfast:glow_jam`): Intermediate ingredient crafted from glow berries for baking.

---

## 2. Advanced Agricultural & Processing Mechanics

These systems were proposed to add depth to crop management, dairy crafting, and garden automation.

### 🧀 Cheese Aging Progression
Currently, players immediately obtain cheese wheels by curdling milk buckets on the griddle. The proposed roadmap suggests a realistic multi-stage dairy cycle:
1. **Soft Curd**: Fresh byproduct of boiling milk on the griddle.
2. **Wax Coating**: Wrapping soft curds in a protective layer (e.g., honey comb/wax).
3. **Aged Cheese Wheel**: Placing the wheel down in the world where it ages over time (changing block state/textures) before being sliced on the butcher block.

### 🐛 Agricultural Pest Control & Fertilizer
* **Crop Pests**: Insect infestations that periodically affect custom crop blocks and herb pots, requiring players to harvest with shears or apply customized pesticides/bone meal treatments.
* **Composting System**: Integrating custom composters utilizing crop waste, plant seeds, and raw ingredients to create high-yield fertilizers.

---

## 3. Workstation Upgrades & Progression

Suggested progression paths for the Griddle workstation.

### 🍳 Tiered Griddles
* **Cast Iron Griddle**: An upgraded griddle providing increased durability, extra cooking slots (e.g., 6 slots instead of 4), or faster cooking times.
* **Reinforced Griddle**: A nether-grade workstation that is blast-proof and unaffected by lava.

---

## 4. Visual & Aesthetic Polish

Visual depth improvements listed in the **v1.0.19 tasks** checklist.

### 📐 3D Voxel Extrusion Modeling
* **3D Food Models**: Replace the current flat 2D planes used to render placed items (bacon, eggs, sausage, bread slices, etc.) on the Griddle and Butcher Block with 3D extruded voxel models.
* **3D Knife Models**: Creating custom Blockbench models for knives when held in hand or visually placed on the Butcher Block.
